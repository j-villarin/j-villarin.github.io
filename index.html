Intro

The design concept of the Vertx Design Studio logo is vertices, or points, that can be connected to make a polygon. The heart of the company's design workflow is an important concept in 3D modeling. It resembles the individual members of a company that has social cohesion. If everybody does their part, everyone can benefit, which also determines the course of the company. The arrows of the design represent the coordinates of the 3D cartesian system, representing the broad scope of work of the company and its diversifying workflows. Providing not only architectural services but also multiple design trades.

Every employee at VertX Design Studio shares a purpose - driven mentality, to build better lives through design. This creates an integrated team of architects, designers, and support staff who are fully invested in our clients mission. The resulting facilities provide personalized environments that enable our clients to succeed.

Work

A nationally recognized design firm, Vertx Design Studio has an unwavering belief in and passion for solving problems and improving lives through the craft of architectural design. We are one firm with one purpose: to serve multiple architecture & design markets while creating healthy, thriving communities.

Our studio offers Sustainable Architectural and Landscape Design & Consultation; Architectural Visualization; Computational Design & Research; Interior Design; Product & Furniture Design; Web Design & Development; Content Strategy; Copywriting/ Editing; Search Engine Optimization; Social Media Creation; Digital design; Brand Messaging; Publication Design; Infographics; Motion Graphics and Video Production.

About

We work at all scales and in all sectors. We create transformative cultural, corporate, residential and designs that transcends modern design. Started in Butuan city, Agusan Del Norte, Philippines. With 400 projects with 100 diligent but ecstatic staffs.

Our passion for our work and the belief that our work matters empowers us to produce the buildings we promise our clients: places where people are born and restored; educated and graduated; worship and marry; work and live.
